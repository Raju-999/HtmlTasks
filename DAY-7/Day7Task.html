Veg Biryani's

  • kaju Dum Biryani

    Kaju Dum Biryani is a rich, flavorful, and aromatic rice dish made with basmati rice, cashew nuts (kaju), and a mix of spices. It’s often served as a special treat or for festive occasions, and is a popular vegetarian alternative to the traditional chicken or mutton biryani. The cashews add a creamy, nutty texture that complements the fragrant rice and spiced gravy.Seemore

  • Paneer Biryani

    Paneer Biryani is a delicious, vegetarian version of the classic biryani, made with soft paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cubes cooked in a fragrant and flavorful rice base with aromatic spices. It’s a perfect dish for vegetarians who love biryani but want a meat-free option. The combination of paneer and the rich biryani spices makes it a hearty and satisfying meal.Seemore

  • Mushroom Biryani

    Mushroom Biryani is a fragrant and delicious vegetarian rice dish made with spiced mushrooms and basmati rice. The mushrooms absorb all the aromatic spices, making this biryani flavorful and rich. It's a great option for mushroom lovers and a perfect addition to any meal or special occasion. Seemore

Non-Veg Biryani's

  • Mutton Biryani

    Mutton Biryani is a classic, flavorful, and aromatic dish made with tender mutton (goat or lamb) pieces, basmati rice, and a blend of spices. The meat is marinated, cooked with fragrant rice, and then layered for a deliciously rich biryani experience. It’s a popular dish in Indian, Pakistani, and Middle Eastern cuisine, often served during special occasions, festivals, or family gatherings.1Seemore

  • chicken Biryani

    Chicken Biryani is a beloved and aromatic dish made with tender chicken pieces, basmati rice, and a blend of fragrant spices. This flavorful one-pot meal is often served at special occasions or family gatherings. There are many regional variations of chicken biryani, but the key to a perfect biryani is tender, well-spiced chicken, fluffy rice, and the right balance of herbs and spices.Seemore
